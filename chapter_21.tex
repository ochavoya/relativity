The vector $A^\mu$ has the covariant divergence $A^\mu_{;\mu}$, which is a scalar. We have
\[
    {A^\mu}_{;\mu} = A^\mu_{,\mu} + \Gamma^\mu_{\nu\mu} A^\nu = A^\mu_{,\mu}+\sqrt{}^{-1}\sqrt{}_{,\nu}A^\nu.
\]
Thus
\begin{equation}
 \label{ecuacion 21.1}
 A^\mu_{;\mu} \sqrt{} = \left( A^\mu \sqrt{} \right)_{,\mu}.
\end{equation}
